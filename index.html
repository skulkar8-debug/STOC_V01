Platform Investment Thesis
The U.S. medical spa market represents a $44.83B fragmented opportunity with sub-5% PE penetration, 40–60% cash-pay EBITDA margins, and no dominant national brand.
Consumer demand is structural and growing. 46% of U.S. consumers increased cosmetic spend in 2024 vs. 2023; Gen Z at 53%.2 Gen Z and Millennials drive 41% of annual wellness spend.2
STOC's STOC framework identifies 11,393 qualified targets across 686 CBSAs, scored across 20+ supply, digital, and ownership signals at 93% classification accuracy.
